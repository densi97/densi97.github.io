About me

My name is Fabian and I'm a Security Consultant.
I am especially interested in the area of Web- and Mobile-Security.
During my studies, I wanted to gain more practical experience and decided to take the Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP). With this certificate, I also knew that I would like to gain more hands-on experience and decided to join a security consultant company where I could constantly face new challenges and projects.
In March 2021, I started my own business with two colleagues and launched my own SaaS product. It has always been a dream of mine to create my own software product. I love developing new features as all it takes is an idea and a laptop.
